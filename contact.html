FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?+

Yes — most work is remote and done online, so location doesn't matter. Payments can be arranged through whatever method works for both sides.

How long does a typical project take?+

A simple website usually takes 3–7 days, design work 1–3 days per batch, and tutoring starts as soon as a schedule is agreed on.

How does payment work?+

Payment terms are agreed on before starting — usually a portion upfront and the rest on delivery for larger projects.

Can I get a free trial before committing?+

Yes, for tutoring a free 15-minute trial session is available. For design or development work, a quick call to discuss scope comes first.
