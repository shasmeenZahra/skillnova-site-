Hi, I'm Shasmeen.

I'm a full-stack developer and AI specialist by trade, a designer and video editor by skill, and a school teacher by profession. Skillnova exists because clients kept needing more than one of these things at once — a website and the branding to go with it, a chatbot and the content around it, or simply someone who could explain a hard subject clearly. So instead of splitting that across different people, I handle it all myself.

On the teaching side, I currently teach Mathematics and other subjects at school, working directly with students day to day. That classroom experience shapes how I approach online tutoring too — clear explanations, real patience, and no rushing a student who needs a concept repeated a different way.

On the technical side, I build with React, Next.js, TypeScript, and Python/LangChain for AI integrations, and handle the design and video work that usually goes alongside a project — logos, social content, and edited video.
